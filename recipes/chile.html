Chile Con Carne

A tasty and easy to make chile

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Mushrooms
  • Fresh chiles
  • Can of chopped tomatos
  • Kidney beans
  • Beef Stock
  • Chile powder
  • Ground cumin
  • Fresh parsley

How to make

  1. Add one large onion and several cloves on garlic to a large casserole dish and allow them to soften for 4-5 minutes
  2. Add the minced beef as well as some chile powder, cumin, salt and pepper and stir until the mince is browned
  3. Add in several chopped carrots, mushrooms and chiles as well as the canned tomatos and kidney beans
  4. Add in roughly 3/4 pint of beef stock and leave to simmer for approx 2 hours
  5. Check the seasoning and adjust as required
  6. Serve with rice and some fresh parsley
