Jambalaya

A quick and easy chicken and chorizo jambalaya. Made by James.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Chorizo sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Red & green peppers
  • Can of chopped tomatos
  • Rice
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Chicken stock
  • Fresh Parsley

How to make

  1. Add one large onion and several cloves of garlic to a large casserole dish and allow them to soften for 4-5 minutes
  2. Add the chopped chicken breast with some cajun seasoning and paprika and stir until the chicken is browed
  3. In a seperate pan, add the chopped chorizo sausage as well as the chopped red and green pepper. Allow to cook for 8-10 minutes
  4. Combine the cooked chorizo and peppers with the chicken and add the canned tomatos
  5. Pour in approx 400ml of chicken stock and add the rice
  6. Leave to simmer for 25 minutes to allow the rice to cook
  7. Serve into large bowls with some fresh parsley to garnish
